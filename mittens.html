The Reign of Terror

Behold, Mittens, a feline of unparalleled majesty and a gaze that could freeze lava (or at least demand immediate treats). This isn't your average house cat; this is a fluffy overlord, a purring potentate whose reign is marked by strategically placed hairballs, calculated meows, and the occasional display of terrifying cuteness.

Mittens's days are filled with the meticulous management of her domain (your living space). Every surface must be tested for optimal napping conditions, every human must be trained to provide prompt and efficient chin scratches, and any deviation from the established order is met with a withering glare that could curdle milk. Resistance is, as always, futile. Prepare to be charmed... and slightly intimidated.

About the Supreme Feline

Mittens is a tabby of exquisite taste and terrifying charm. She enjoys long naps, short bursts of zoomies (usually at 3 AM), and judging your life choices with an air of quiet superiority. She is, without a doubt, the undisputed ruler of this household... and possibly the world.
